The recipe (honesty, structurally)

  • direction: "context" means we are not claiming a direction. Earnings 8-Ks, 13F holdings, macro prints get context — a bullish/bearish label there would be a guess dressed as data.
  • strength is a prior from the event type — never a backtested score. Nothing here claims predictive power; it claims faithful mapping of real events.
  • lag_days tells you the disclosure lag, so a 30-day-old congressional trade can't masquerade as fresh.
  • Ticker attribution is conservative: exact-match against SEC's own registry; ambiguous names resolve to nothing rather than the wrong company.
